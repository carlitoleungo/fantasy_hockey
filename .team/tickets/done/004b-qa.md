### Issues found

**Note (not a bug — deviation from letter of AC2):**
The ticket specifies "requests.post is mocked and `call_count == 0`." The engineer instead patches `web.routes.auth.exchange_code` and checks its `call_count`. The intent — confirming no live Yahoo call is made — is fully satisfied by this approach, and the engineer explains the reason in the handoff: `exchange_code` reads env vars unavailable in CI. The outcome is identical: zero outbound network calls during the test. This is an acceptable implementation choice, not a defect.
